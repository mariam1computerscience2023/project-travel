Popular Distination In Greece

Athens, Greece's capital, is rich in history with landmarks like the Acropolis and Parthenon. It's a vibrant city with lively markets, museums, and a dynamic nightlife, perfect for culture enthusiast.

Santorini is famous for its stunning sunsets, white-washed buildings, and blue-domed churches. Visitors love its caldera views, beautiful beaches, and romantic villages like Oia and Fira.

Mykonos is known for its lively nightlife, luxury resorts, and iconic windmills. The island offers beautiful beaches, vibrant beach clubs, and charming streets in Mykonos Town.

Meteora features monasteries perched on towering rock formations, offering breathtaking views and a spiritual retreat. It's a UNESCO site ideal for hiking and exploring nature.

Greece News

Thessaloniki

Mon, May 25th 2020

Located in the northern region of Macedonia, Thessaloniki is Greece's second-largest city and a cultural hub. Known for its rich history, it boasts significant landmarks such as the Rotunda, an early Christian monument, and the White Tower, a symbol of the city. Thessaloniki is also renowned for its lively cultural scene, hosting numerous festivals, including the Thessaloniki International Film Festival and various music and art events that draw both locals and visitors alike.

Corfu

Mon, May 25th 2020

Corfu, an island in the Ionian Sea, is celebrated for its stunning landscapes, combining lush green hills with crystal-clear waters. The island's Venetian architecture, a remnant of centuries of Venetian rule, is most evident in the Old Town of Corfu, a UNESCO World Heritage site. The town's narrow, winding streets are lined with historic buildings, charming cafes, and shops. Corfu also offers a rich cultural experience with its museums, churches, and the famous Easter celebrations, which are among the most vibrant in Greece.

Nafplio

Mon, May 25th 2020

Nafplio, located in the Peloponnese region, is often described as one of Greece's most beautiful towns. As the first capital of modern Greece, Nafplio is steeped in history, with its neoclassical architecture, Venetian fortresses, and cobblestone streets. The Palamidi Fortress, perched high on a hill overlooking the town, offers stunning views of the surrounding area. Nafplio's romantic ambiance, with its picturesque old town, vibrant squares, and seaside promenade, makes it a favorite destination for both Greek and international tourists.

Zagori

Mon, May 25th 2020

Zagori, a region in northwestern Greece, is renowned for its natural beauty and traditional stone-built villages. The area is part of the Pindus Mountain range, with the Vikos Gorge, one of the deepest gorges in the world, being a highlight for visitors. Zagori is an ideal destination for those who love nature and outdoor activities, offering numerous hiking trails through forests, over arched stone bridges, and along mountain streams. The villages of Zagori, known as the "Zagorochoria," retain their traditional character, with stone houses, slate roofs, and cobbled streets, offering a glimpse into a timeless way of life.
